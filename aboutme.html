About Me

I am a Web Design student with an interest in creating simple, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I am currently learning HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript, and improving my design and development skills through academic projects.

I completed my secondary education at Pannala Maha Vidyalaya and Seelananda Maha Vidyalaya. I completed my G.C.E. Advanced Level studies at Rajasinghe Central College. I am currently pursuing my studies at Uva Wellassa University.

My aim is to successfully complete my degree with strong academic performance and graduate with a First Class Honours. I intend to build a career in the field of computer networking, developing skills in network design, administration, and security, while maintaining a balanced and sustainable lifestyle.
